 # Amazon Sales Dashboard (Power BI)

A 2-page Power BI dashboard visualizing Amazon e-commerce sales data from **2020 to 2024**.

---

## Page 1 — Overview

High-level summary of total revenue, orders, and shipping across countries, categories, and cities.

### KPI Cards
| Metric | Value |
|---|---|
| Total Amount | 91.31M |
| Avg Amount | 918.36 |
| Total Orders | 99K |
| Shipping Cost | 736.65K |

### Charts
- **Total Amount by Category** — Electronics leads (15.5M), followed by Sports & Outdoors, Clothing, Books, Toys & Games, and Home & Kitchen
- **Total Amount by Years** — Peak at 18.53M (2020), dip in 2021, recovery to 18.51M (2023), decline to 17.65M in 2024
- **Total Amount by Country** — USA dominates (64M), India 14M, Canada/UK/Australia 4–5M
- **Total Amount by City** — Charlotte highest at 4.73M; treemap shows top 18+ cities

### Filters
Country · Category · City · Order Date (01/01/2020 – 19/12/2024)

---

## Page 2 — Products

Detailed breakdown of order status, payment methods, shipping cost by country, and brand performance.

### KPI Cards
| Metric | Value |
|---|---|
| Total Discount | 7.38K |
| Shipping Cost | 736.65K |
| Total Tax | 6.81M |

### Charts
- **Order Status** — Delivered = 68.01M (majority); Shipped 14M; Pending 3.71M; Cancelled 2.76M; Returned (smallest)
- **Payment Method** — Credit Card 31.95M; Debit Card 18.42M; Amazon Pay 13.82M; Net Banking 13.63M; UPI 9.01M; Cash on Delivery 4.49M
- **Shipping Cost by Country** — US = 516K; India = 111K; Canada 43K; UK 36K; Australia 31K
- **Top Brands** — Zenith (694K), ReadMore, CoreTech, Apex, KiddoFun, UrbanStyle, BrightLux, HomeEase, FitLife, NexPro

### Filters
Country · Category · City · Order Date
