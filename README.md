# Amazon Sales Dashboard (Power BI)

A 2-page Power BI dashboard visualizing Amazon e-commerce sales data from **2020 to 2024**.

---

## Page 1 — Overview

High-level summary of total revenue, orders, and shipping across countries, categories, and cities.

### KPI Cards
| Metric | Value |
|---|---|
| Total Amount | 91.31M |
| Avg Amount | 918.36 |
| Total Orders | 99K |
| Shipping Cost | 736.65K |

### Charts
- **Total Amount by Category** — Electronics leads (15.5M), followed by Sports & Outdoors, Clothing, Books, Toys & Games, and Home & Kitchen
- **Total Amount by Years** — Peak at 18.53M (2020), dip in 2021, recovery to 18.51M (2023), decline to 17.65M in 2024
- **Total Amount by Country** — USA dominates (64M), India 14M, Canada/UK/Australia 4–5M
- **Total Amount by City** — Charlotte highest at 4.73M; treemap shows top 18+ cities

### Filters
Country · Category · City · Order Date (01/01/2020 – 19/12/2024)
